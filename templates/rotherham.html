Rotherham Pride 2021

Location: All Saints Square

10th July
Time: TBA

Rotherham Pride will be held on Saturday 10th July 2021.

The event is filled with fun activities and entertainment. With the 2020 cancellation, there is hope for a better 2021 Rotherham pride event for the whole community.
The event’s entrance remains free for all and it aims at promoting the LGBTQIA+ community and creating awareness of emerging issues.

A parade is scheduled to begin from All Saints square, which will be concluded at the same place after moving around the town. There will be African Rotherham drummers to end the parade event with special renditions. The main festival site will be situated around the Clifton Park Bandstand with timings TBC.
